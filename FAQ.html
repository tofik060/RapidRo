FAQ!

What is Reverse Osmosis. ?

Reverse Osmosis (RO) is the reversal ofthe natural flow of osmosis. In water purification systems, reverse osmosis is used to separate the salts and other heavy metals from water.

What is Rapid R.O. ?

Rapid RO is domestic RO system that uses reverse osmosis to remove TDS, microbiological contaminants, heavy metals etc., dissolved in water. The result is pure, safe water that tastes good and is healthy to drink. Rapid has 6 stage RO purification technology.

How do I Know Which Purifies i Need For Office/Home ?

Water treatment is like any other treatment of your health - your first need to know what's wrong with your water before you treat it. Rapid the leader in water purification for years, has a range of product using different technologies - ranging from RO UV and UF with optional attachment to treat excess iron, hardness and arsenic in water. Hence its essential that you consult a Rapid water specialists who will test your water and then guide you into making the right decision.

Which is More Better Technology ?

Yes, RO is better technology than Ultraviolet (UV), if your water has microbial organic, physical, TDS, heavy metals and other pollutants, you will need to install a RO system.
